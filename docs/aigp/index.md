## Design Intent

- Preserve truthful evaluation reporting over optimistic progression.
- Treat curriculum gates as explicit policy checks, not implicit behavior.
- Keep training artifacts queryable by both humans and automation.
- Keep qualifier readiness measurable from deterministic run artifacts.
